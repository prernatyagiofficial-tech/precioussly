How to Measure Ring Size

Method 1: Use a String or Paper

  1. Wrap a piece of string or paper around the base of your finger.
  2. Mark the point where the ends meet with a pen.
  3. Measure the string or paper with a ruler in millimeters (mm).
  4. Divide by 3.14 (or match to a standard ring size chart) to get the diameter.

Method 2: Measure an Existing Ring

  • Take a ring that fits you well.
  • Measure the inside diameter in millimeters using a ruler.
  • Match the diameter to our standard ring sizes.
